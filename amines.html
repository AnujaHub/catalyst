Organic Nitrogen Compounds

Diazotization Reaction

C₆H₅NH₂ + NaNO₂ + HCl

Answer: Aniline reacts with sodium nitrite and hydrochloric acid to form a diazonium salt, which can further undergo substitution reactions.

Hofmann Bromamide Degradation

Amide + Br₂ + NaOH

Answer: In this reaction, an amide is degraded to a primary amine with one less carbon atom.
