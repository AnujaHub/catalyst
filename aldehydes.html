Aldehydes, Ketones, and Carboxylic Acids

Aldol Condensation

Acetaldehyde + Base

Answer: The reaction forms β-hydroxy aldehyde (aldol), which can undergo dehydration to form α,β-unsaturated carbonyl compound (enone or enal).

Reduction of Aldehydes and Ketones

R–CHO + H₂ →

Answer: Aldehydes and ketones are reduced to alcohols by reducing agents such as NaBH₄ or LiAlH₄.

Reduction of Carboxylic Acids

R–COOH + H₂ →

Answer: Carboxylic acids are reduced to aldehydes or alcohols using reducing agents like LiAlH₄.
